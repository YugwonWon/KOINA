
usage: 	Mtargets [options] win1 lo hi maxerr win2 mind minr < [F0 values]

win1:		cible window length
lo:		F0 threshold
hi:		F0 ceiling
maxerr:		maximum error
win2:		reduc window length
mind:		minimal distance
minr:		minimal frequency ratio

options:		--non-elim-glitch

F0 values, sampled at 10 ms, in ASCII file.
